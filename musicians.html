Balasubramanya Sharma

Vocal

Vid. Balasubramanya Sharma is one of the most sought after vocalist for Bharatanatyam. He has extensively toured US, UK, Europe, Canada and Singapore for Arengetrams, Lec dem and Music concerts. He has been conferred with many prestigous awards and Titles. KarnatakaKalashree, Sangeetharnava, Kalyogi etc. to name a few. He has composed lyrics for various dance items, composed music for Dance Ballets, Varnams, Padams, Javalis, Thillanas, etc.

Gurumurthy G

Mridangam

Vid. G Gurumurthy is one of the most senior mridangist for Bharatanatyam. He has extensively toured US, UK, Europe, Canada and Singapore for Arengetrams, Lec Dem. He has been conferred with many prestigious awards and Titles. Aaryabhatta award, KarnatakaKalashree, Layakala Choodamani, Gokulam Puraskar, Ananya Puraskar, Kalayogi, Kalashreshta etc .. to name a few. In July 1986, he created a record by accompanying dancer KRS Prasanna on the Mridangam for 24 hours non-stop! He has composed lyrics and Rythm pattern for Dance Ballets, Varnams, Padams, Javalis, Thillanas, etc.
